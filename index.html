About Me

My name is Heidi Jiang. I'm a second-year student at Northeastern University, and my major is Data Science and Business Administration with a concentration in Finance. My hometown is Foster City, California.

I participated in the N.U.in program during my first semester at Northeastern, so I got the opportunity to study abroad in Thessaloniki, Greece. I enjoyed exploring the culture, food, and architecture in Greece. I also met many of my closest friends through the N.U.in program.

I've been exploring my interests in machine learning, neural networks, and web development. Some of my non-CS-related hobbies include Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, cooking, philosophy, astronomy, digital art, and learning languages.

I'm currently a teaching assistant for Financial Accounting and Reporting at the D'Amore-McKim School of Business. I grade exams, create homework assignments, and host weekly office hours to answer questions from students.

I've also been interning as an LLM Engineer at Fengyun Vision, LLC. I'm developing an AI sales chatbot for E-commerce companies that will eventually be integrated with Facebook Messenger. This chatbot relies on a RAG pipeline that pulls context from previous conversations between human sales representatives and their customers.

Most recently, my friend offered me a position in app development for a soccer league.
